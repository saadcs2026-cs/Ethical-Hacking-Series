# EP 6 — What is a VPN?

## Definition
VPN stands for Virtual Private Network.
Hides your real IP and encrypts all traffic.

## What it Does
- Hides your real IP address
- Encrypts all your internet traffic
- Makes you appear from different location
- Protects you on public WiFi
- Keeps your browsing private

## How it Works
Without VPN:
Your Data → Internet → Anyone can see it!

With VPN:
Your Data → Encrypted Tunnel → 
VPN Server → Internet → 100% Private!

## When to Use VPN
- On public WiFi in cafes or airports
- When accessing sensitive accounts
- To protect your privacy online
- When using unsecured networks
