# EP 15 — What is Subnetting?

## Definition
Dividing a large network into smaller
more manageable networks.
Like dividing a city into neighbourhoods!

## Why Subnetting?
- Makes networks faster
- More secure — limits attack spread
- Easier to manage
- Used in every company network

## Simple Example
Full network: 192.168.1.0

Subnet 1: 192.168.1.0 — 192.168.1.63
Subnet 2: 192.168.1.64 — 192.168.1.127
Subnet 3: 192.168.1.128 — 192.168.1.191

Each subnet isolated from others!

## Security Benefit
If one subnet is hacked —
other subnets stay safe!
