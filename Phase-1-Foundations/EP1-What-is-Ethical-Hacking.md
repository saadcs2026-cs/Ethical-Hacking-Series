# EP 1 — What is Ethical Hacking?

## Definition
Ethical hacking means legally breaking 
into systems to find weaknesses before 
bad hackers do.

## Key Points
- Also called penetration testing
- Done with full permission of the owner
- Goal is to fix vulnerabilities not exploit them
- Used by companies, governments and banks

## What Ethical Hackers Do
- Test websites for security holes
- Find weak passwords in systems
- Simulate real cyber attacks safely
- Write reports on vulnerabilities found
