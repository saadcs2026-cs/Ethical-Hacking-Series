# EP 2 — Types of Hackers

## White Hat Hacker
- Work legally with permission
- Help companies find vulnerabilities
- Also called ethical hackers
- Earn certifications like CEH

## Black Hat Hacker
- Hack without permission
- Steal data and money
- Install malware and viruses
- Face serious jail time

## Grey Hat Hacker
- Hack without permission but mean no harm
- Often report vulnerabilities after finding them
- Not fully legal but not fully criminal
