# EP 3 — Common Cyber Attacks

## Phishing
Fake emails tricking you into giving 
personal info. Most common attack worldwide!

## Malware
Malicious software installed without 
your knowledge. Includes viruses, 
ransomware, spyware.

## Brute Force
Trying millions of passwords automatically
until one works.

## Man in the Middle
Attacker secretly intercepts your 
communication. Most dangerous on public WiFi!

## SQL Injection
Injecting malicious code into website 
databases. Steals entire user databases!

## DDoS Attack
Flooding a server with traffic until 
it crashes.

## How to Stay Safe
- Use strong unique passwords
- Never click suspicious links
- Avoid public WiFi without VPN
- Keep software updated
- Enable two factor authentication
